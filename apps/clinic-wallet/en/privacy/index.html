Privacy Policy

Clinic Wallet

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Prefrontal Ltd is committed to protecting the privacy of users of the Clinic Wallet application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and the rights available to you regarding your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture (when signing in with Google)

Phone Number (Optional)

You may choose to add your phone number within the application. Providing a phone number is entirely optional and is not required to use the app.

Clinic Data

  • Services
  • Pricing
  • Deductions
  • Financial records
  • Subscription information

Device Information

We may collect technical device identifiers to help protect user accounts, prevent abuse, and improve the security of our services.

Performance Data

Technical logs and crash reports may be collected to improve application performance and reliability.

3. Why We Collect Your Information

  • Provide and operate the application.
  • Create and manage your account.
  • Manage subscriptions.
  • Operate the referral and Coins system.
  • Prevent fraud and misuse.
  • Improve application performance.
  • Provide customer support when needed.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only when necessary to provide our services through:

  • Supabase – Database hosting and management.
  • Google – Google Sign-In authentication.
  • Apple App Store and Google Play – Payment and subscription processing.

5. Payments

All payments and subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not access, store, or process your payment card information.

6. Coins

  • Coins are earned only through the referral program.
  • Coins cannot be purchased.
  • Coins cannot be exchanged for cash.
  • Coins have no monetary value outside the application.

7. Data Security

Your data is protected using encrypted connections (TLS) and stored securely using Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS).

8. Data Retention

We retain your information while your account remains active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be permanently removed within 14 days, unless retention of certain information is required by law.

9. Your Rights

  • Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correct or update your information.
  • Delete your account and personal data.
  • Contact us regarding any privacy-related questions.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When significant changes are made, the "Last updated" date will be revised.
