How to Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account directly from within the application:

Settings → Delete Account

What Happens After Deleting Your Account?

  • Your account will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.
  • Your personal information and clinic data will be deleted from our systems within 14 days.
  • We may retain certain information where required by applicable laws or legal obligations.
  • Any remaining Coins balance will be permanently forfeited and cannot be restored.
  • Deleting your account does not entitle you to a refund for any active or previously paid subscription.

Cancel Your Subscription Before Deleting Your Account

If you have an active subscription, you should cancel its automatic renewal through your app store before deleting your account to avoid future charges.

  • Apple App Store: Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions
  • Google Play: Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions
