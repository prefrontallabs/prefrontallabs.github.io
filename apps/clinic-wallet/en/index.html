Clinic Wallet

Create a wallet. Drag your services. Know your income.

Developed by Prefrontal Labs

About

Clinic Wallet is designed for professionals who provide fixed-price services, including doctors, consultants, coaches, and other service providers.

Instead of manually calculating income, simply create your services once, then build a work wallet by dragging completed services into it.

Each service can include its own price, deduction, and emoji, allowing Clinic Wallet to calculate your gross income, deductions, and net income automatically.

How It Works

  1. Create your services once.
  2. Set the service price, optional deduction, and choose an emoji.
  3. Open a wallet whenever you start working.
  4. Drag completed services into the wallet.
  5. Keep the wallet open for a single session, a full day, a month, or as long as you need.
  6. Close the wallet whenever you finish and instantly view your income summary.

Features

  • Wallet-based income tracking
  • Drag & Drop service recording
  • Automatic deduction calculation
  • Automatic net income calculation
  • Unlimited work wallets
  • Flexible wallet duration
  • Monthly income overview
  • Referral rewards
  • Secure cloud synchronization
