Refund Policy

Clinic Wallet

Last updated: July 2026

1. Payments and Refunds

All subscriptions and payments are processed exclusively through Apple App Store or Google Play.

Accordingly, all refund requests are governed by the refund policies of the app store through which the purchase was made.

You can review the official refund policies here:

  • Apple App Store
  • Google Play

Prefrontal Ltd is not able to issue direct refunds for purchases made through Apple App Store or Google Play.

2. Coins

The application may provide Coins or similar rewards according to the rules displayed within the application.

  • Coins cannot be purchased with money.
  • Coins cannot be exchanged for cash.
  • Coins may be redeemed for eligible benefits or subscription rewards as described within the application.
  • If a subscription payment is refunded, Prefrontal Ltd reserves the right to revoke any Coins or benefits associated with that purchase.

3. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel the automatic renewal of your subscription at any time through your Apple App Store or Google Play account settings.

Your subscription will remain active until the end of the paid billing period.

4. Account Deletion

Deleting your account does not entitle you to a refund for any active or previously paid subscription.

If you wish to stop future charges, you must cancel the subscription through your app store before the next renewal date.
