How Can We Help?

Clinic Wallet is designed for service providers, including doctors, consultants, coaches, and other professionals, to help manage and track their income in a simple and efficient way.

If you experience any issue while using the application, we're here to help.

Common Issues

Sign-in Problems

  • Make sure your device is connected to the Internet.
  • Sign in using the same Google account you originally registered with.
  • Restart the application and try again.

Subscription Issues

  • Verify your subscription status through Apple App Store or Google Play.
  • Ensure you are signed in with the same account used to purchase the subscription.

Missing Data or Incorrect Reports

  • Contact us with a detailed description of the issue.
  • If possible, attach screenshots.
  • Please include your device model and app version if available.

Before Contacting Us

Please include the following information whenever possible:

  • The email address used to sign in.
  • A brief description of the issue.
  • The steps required to reproduce the problem.
  • Your application version (if known).

Providing this information helps us resolve your issue more quickly.
